FAQ

Common questions from new users.

Who is this for?

Patients, carers, older adults, families, and anyone who wants a simpler way to keep key medical details together on their iPhone.

Is this a replacement for medical advice?

No. MEdical Info is an organisation tool for personal reference and sharing. Always follow advice from your doctor, pharmacist, hospital, or other qualified clinician.

What should I put in it first?

Start with medications, allergies, conditions, emergency contact details, and the doctor information you most often need in appointments or forms.

Where is my information stored?

Most medical information stays on the device in the current version. See the privacy policy for the exact details and the optional address lookup exception.

Can I use it for someone I care for?

Yes, if you have their permission and are helping manage their details. The app is especially useful when one person helps keep appointments, medications, and key contacts organised.
