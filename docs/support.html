MEdical Info

Support

MEdical Info is a local-first app for storing and showing important personal medical information from a phone. This page is the public support page used for the App Store listing.

What the App Does

  • stores personal details and emergency contacts
  • tracks medications, allergies, conditions, surgeries, and providers
  • shows a clean doctor-facing summary
  • supports local export and import backup
  • supports medication reminders and dose logging

Quick Help

Moving to a new phone? Use Export in Settings on the old phone, then Import on the new phone.

Want extra privacy? Turn on app lock and screen privacy protection in Settings.

Need something for a doctor? Use Doctor View or export the doctor PDF from Settings.

Reminder Notes

Reminder times are based on the wake and sleep window saved in Settings. The app is designed to avoid overnight reminders outside those hours.

Privacy

Most medical information is stored locally on the device. For full details, please read the privacy policy.
