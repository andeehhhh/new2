The real problem

The issue is not missing information. It is having the right information in the wrong places.

Most people already have their medical information, but it is scattered across notes, screenshots, paper, memory, text threads, old forms, and discharge letters. MEdical Info brings the essentials together into one practical medical card.

FAQ

Common questions from new users.

Who is this for?

Patients, carers, older adults, families, and anyone who wants a simpler way to keep key medical details together on their iPhone.

Is this a replacement for medical advice?

No. MEdical Info is an organisation tool for personal reference and sharing. Always follow advice from your doctor, pharmacist, hospital, or other qualified clinician.

Where is my information stored?

Most medical information stays on the device in the current version. Read the privacy policy for the exact details and the optional address lookup exception.
